Customer communications note - ops lead

- For launch-week customers, provide a date-specific ETA in the first response and avoid soft language like "soon" or "shortly".
- If a shipment is split, include exact SKU counts in the update so store teams can plan shelf coverage.
- For billing disputes, acknowledge the hold reason, provide correction ETA, and copy finance in the same thread.
- If payment is held due to invoice mismatch, send corrected invoice within 24 hours and log the blocker in collections notes.
